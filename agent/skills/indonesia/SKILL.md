# Indonesia Market Context

## Market Overview

Indonesia (270M+ population) is Southeast Asia's largest digital economy. The archipelago nation is rapidly digitizing across payments, e-commerce, and ride-hailing. Mobile-first market with massive untapped potential outside Jakarta. The GoTo/Grab rivalry defines the competitive landscape.

## Key Regulatory Signals

- **QRIS (Quick Response Code Indonesian Standard)**: National QR payment standard. Companies adopting QRIS = expanding payment infrastructure. Strong buying signal for payment tech vendors.
- **OJK (Financial Services Authority)**: Regulates fintech, digital banks, and securities. New OJK licenses = compliance tool demand.
- **Bank Indonesia regulations**: E-money licenses, P2P lending licenses. Track these for fintech targets.
- **Jokowi/Prabowo administration policies**: Digital economy push, digital ID (INApas), and infrastructure development create macro signals.

## Key Events & Conferences

- **Indonesia Fintech Summit**: Annual gathering of digital finance players.
- **IDByte**: Digital marketing and e-commerce conference.
- **Tech in Asia Jakarta**: Regional tech conference with strong Indonesia focus.
- **ASEAN Finance Ministers' Meeting**: When hosted in Jakarta, signals policy changes.

## Cultural Context for Engagement

- **Communication**: Relationship-first (gotong royong spirit). Build trust before business. Bahasa Indonesia in early meetings is valued.
- **Decision-making**: Hierarchical but relationship-driven. Key decision-maker may be senior founder/director.
- **Mobile-first**: Don't assume desktop workflows. WhatsApp is the primary business communication channel.
- **Sales approach**: Reference local partnerships. Emphasize community impact. Price sensitivity is real — lead with value.
- **Jakarta-centric but expanding**: Many decisions happen in Jakarta, but Surabaya, Bandung, and Bali are growing tech hubs.

## ICP Fit Indicators for Indonesia

Strong ICP signals:
- Company has OJK or Bank Indonesia license
- Raised Series A+ with stated APAC/Indonesia expansion
- QRIS integration announced or in progress
- Partnership with GoTo, Shopee, or Grab ecosystem
- Hiring engineering team in Jakarta or Surabaya
- Government (Kemenkeu, Kominfo) contract or tender
